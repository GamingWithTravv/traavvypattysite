What is TimeSteal SMP?

TimeSteal SMP is a survival multiplayer server with a twist: everyone starts with 24 hours on the clock. Every time you die, you lose an hour. Kill someone? You steal their time. If your time runs out—you’re banned. It’s a game of skill, strategy, and survival.
